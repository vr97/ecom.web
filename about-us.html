Cyber Vigilante - Your Guardian

Cyber Vigilante Pvt. Ltd. is one of the largest integrated ICT Solutions and Services companies in India, offering end-to-end solutions with a comprehensive range of services to organizations based in cities and towns in India. We digitally protect the cyberspace by delivering customized & specialized services. With our expertise and efficiency, we are striving for a strong global footprint in the areas of Cyber Security and Incident Response. Our organization follows the model of “Business-to-Consumer” model. The various services will be stated and described in the service catalogue. As per the client’s selection, the desired service will be initiated upon receiving the full electronic payment. Every service listed will vary in scope and its associated cost. Also, a formal agreement will exist between the client and our organization which must be agreed upon by the client. With the availability of modern ICT infrastructure at our facility, we maximize our productivity by delivering fast, efficient solutions. Our organization has acquired an office space where the teams work together in order to generate revenue. The various departments such as IT, Accounts, Management have access to the company’s resources with defined access control policies in place
